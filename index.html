About Our Company

Coco's Chocolatiers started back in 2008 when two college seniors were procrastinating from studying and started making their own chocolate. They soon realized that they had quite the knack for it and began selling their candies to other students.

After graduating the two students decided to make a living out of their candy creations. They started selling their creations out of other small businesses and after a few years of experimentation and struggle, opened the doors to their first store in Westboro.

Coco's Chocolatiers aim to stick to their roots; fun, feel good chocolates that help in stressful times. They seek to help students through their tough college years by keeping a staff of over 90% students and providing scholarships and grants.
